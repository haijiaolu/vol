# Alan Kay（艾伦·凯）

Alan Kay (1940-)，美国计算机科学家。

:::info

> [!quote]
>
> People who are really serious about software should make their own hardware. -- Alan Kay
>
> > (真正认真对待软件的人应该制造自己的硬件。)

:::
